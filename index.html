About Mr. Mahendra Singh

Mahendra Singh is the disciple of Vedmurti Taponishat Pandit Sriram Sharma Acharya. His Guru Sriram Sharma Acharya ji came to his house and accepted him as his disciple.

Under his guru's presence & guidance in Shantikunj, Haridwar ashram, Mahendra singh has gone through a number of satras, including Sanskar Karmkand, Ramayan Katha, Srimad Bhagwat Katha and Pragya Puran Katha during 1975-1978.

After that he dedicated his life to spread his guru's message to the world through Pragya Puran Katha.
